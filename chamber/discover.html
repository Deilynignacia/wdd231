Discover Valparaíso

The Gem of the Pacific: Valparaíso

Valparaíso, a UNESCO World Heritage site, is more than just a port city; it's a treasure of culture, history, and colorful chaos. Its hills are crowned with thousands of houses of joyful colors and colonial arquitecture, accessible by funiculars. This page showcases some of the key spots for visitors and prospective members of this chamber, offering a glimpse into the generally unveiled potential of the "Jewel of the Pacific."
